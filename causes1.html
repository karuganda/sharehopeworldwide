Adoption, Fostering & Children Care

We are not an orphanage but If you want to help a child by adoption. We are happy to guide you through the whole process. We identify the most vulnerable children and help them get famillies where they will get a better life.

Food Program

Food is crucial to the survival of any human being. It is so sad that some of us are extra food while others starve. Some of the donations we get in money form is used to buy food for the most unfortunate. Also we link our donors to the ones in need so that they donate and interact directly.

Meditation Services

Rwanda is providing one of the most affordable health services through "Mutuelle de Sante" program but some of us are still not able to afford that. At Alias we help those most unfortunate to get basic medication services with the donations we get.

Education program

We believe that the only way to eradicate poverty is through education. By giving a donation or becoming a sponsor for a child, you will help us give a poor child proper care and education

Disability program

Prosthetics and special needs for disabled can be somewhat expensive and poor famillies have hard time affording the right services. By donating you can help a disabled child to have a normal life with the right health care services

Sheltering Program

Even after all the good programs the government has put in place we still get some children going to the street due to different reasons, Some due to poverty or home abuse or any other reasons. At Alias we believe that with the donation you give we can get a better shelter than streets.

Aid.

We would welcome volunteers who would like to create something permanent and aren't afraid of some heavy lifting!.
