A Few Words About Us

We want to help as much as 0 People

Our mission is to support, enable and empower at-risk orphaned children to become self-reliant adults

We envision a world without poverty in which all children have the freedom and opportunities to pursue happiness and maximize their potential.

Share Hope Worldwide believes in development, not aid, as development at the grassroots level empowers children to rise to their own giftedness and break the cycle of poverty. Education frees people from bondage. It is every child's right to be grow up properly and determine their own destiny. Share Hope Worldwide embraces diversity and supports people of all ages, ethnicities, nationalities, religions, genders, or socioeconomic statuses.
